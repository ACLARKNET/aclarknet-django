    Our company history

    In 2004, Alex Clark began doing business as (DBA) ACLARK.NET; a registered trade name in Maryland, USA. His first client was a company with a Plone website in London, UK.

    In 2007, Amy Clark created ACLARK.NET, LLC; a limited liability corporation (LLC) in Maryland, USA. Our client list began to grow.

    In 2008, Amy Clark created ZPUGDC, Inc; a non-profit 501(c)(3) corporation in Maryland, USA. With this organization we hosted Plone Conference 2008.

    In 2014, we reached a milestone: 10 years in business.
